Indices

Index means a theoretical portfolio of stocks that are an indicator of a particular market or sector.

Investors use indices to track overall market movements and performance because indices provide them with a broader view of current trends.

Determining the best investment in an index requires a valuation of its underlying stocks. Particular indices represent specific stocks or sectors of the market.

Trading indices require the latest analytics and real-time data to be able to make buy and sell decisions.
